Our Mission

The Youths Aglow Campaign aims to reach and engage over 1.3 billion youths globally with Rhapsody of Realities across nations, cities, campuses, schools, and youth communities worldwide. We seek to impact the lives of young people, preparing them for the rapture of the church.

    Reachout World Day - Campaign Goals

    Transformative goals to impact young lives through Rhapsody of Realities

    Reach over 1.3 Billion Youth

    Our primary goal is to reach and impact over 1.3 billion young people globally.

    • Target audience: Youth aged 21-35 years
    • Focus on campus ministries, university students, and youth organizations
    • Empower young people to become ambassadors of positive change

    Global Distribution

    Our goal is to ensure widespread distribution of Rhapsody of Realities:

    • Successful distribution on ReachOut World Day
    • Reach diverse communities across different countries
    • Collaborate with local partners, and multiple youth groups for effective distribution

    Youth Empowerment

    Our ultimate goal is to empower and transform young lives through the gospel.

    • Inspire youth to become active participants in the campaign
    • Provide spiritual guidance and support through Rhapsody of Realities
    • Create a global network of empowered young leaders
